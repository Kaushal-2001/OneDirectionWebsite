One Direction

About

Horan, Malik, Payne, Styles and Tomlinson signed up as solo candidates for The X Factor in 2010 . In the end they couldn't progress to the 'Boys' category, but still on stage they were suggested to continue as a group, to which they agreed. Their coach became Simon Cowell . After four weeks, they were Cowell's last act to compete. One Direction finished third. After The X Factor leaked their single Forever young , which would have been released if they had won. Social media, in combination with a sophisticated marketing strategy, subsequently created a real worldwide hype around the band

Their first single What makes you beautiful was released on September 11, 2011 and reached number 1 in the UK Singles Chart the following week .Their second single Gotta be you was released on November 14 and their first album Up all night was released on November 21, 2011 . On February 21, 2012, One Direction won the Brit Award 'British Single' for their single What makes you beautiful .A month later, on March 21, 2012, their album Up all night hit #1 on the US Billboard 200 , just one day after its release there. They broke a record with that.
